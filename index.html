Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become an Ilustrado?
Our basic requirement is at least 1m total Fame (unlocked Faction Warfare), has a Discord for comms, and active in-game.
What are the Guild's content and primetime?
The Guild's primetime is usually after maintenance, or 12 UTC onwards. For the content, we do Core hunting, Open world chests, Static Fame Farm, and BZ Outposts. Sometimes, we also venture the roads in search for loots / pvp.
How much is the Guild's Tax?
The tax is at 10%, but if you exceed 200k, the excess will be refundable.
How does the guild regears work?
Regear eligibility will be applicable if member has 1300 IP, and at least Tier 4.2 Faction Cape equipped. This could be changed if specified by the officer. The items that will be regeared for you is an item equivalent of your lost item or a Tier 8 Non-Artifact / Tier 6 Artifact Item.
Any Mandatory CTAs? ZvZ?
As of now, the guild has no mandatory CTAs, and we will do our best to keep it that way. As for the ZvZ content, we are still in rebuilding stage, so there are no enough members for the type of content. However, the guild is open for the idea of being a ZvZ guild in the near future.
